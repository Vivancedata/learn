# AI Foundations for Professionals

Welcome to AI Foundations - a comprehensive course designed to build your AI literacy from the ground up. Whether you're a business professional, developer, or lifelong learner, this course will equip you with the knowledge and practical skills to navigate the AI revolution.

## Learning Outcomes

- Understand core AI concepts including machine learning, neural networks, and large language models
- Master prompt engineering techniques for effective AI interactions
- Evaluate AI tools and choose the right ones for your needs
- Apply AI ethically and responsibly in professional contexts
- Build practical AI-powered workflows and applications
- Understand AI limitations, biases, and safety considerations
- Plan and implement AI projects from conception to deployment

## Prerequisites

- Basic computer literacy
- Familiarity with web browsers and online tools
- No programming experience required (though helpful for some advanced topics)
- Curiosity and willingness to experiment

## Course Structure

This course is organized into 7 sections covering everything from AI fundamentals to practical implementation:

### Section 1: Understanding AI
Get a solid foundation in what AI is, how it works, and where it's heading.

### Section 2: Large Language Models
Deep dive into LLMs like ChatGPT, Claude, and others - the AI tools transforming how we work.

### Section 3: Prompt Engineering
Master the art and science of communicating effectively with AI systems.

### Section 4: AI Ethics & Responsibility
Navigate the critical ethical considerations of AI deployment.

### Section 5: Practical AI Applications
Explore real-world use cases across industries and functions.

### Section 6: Building with AI
Get hands-on experience integrating AI into workflows and applications.

### Section 7: AI Strategy & Future
Develop strategic thinking about AI adoption and prepare for what's next.

## Time Commitment

- **Total Duration**: 25 hours
- **Recommended Pace**: 3-4 hours per week
- **Course Length**: 6-8 weeks at recommended pace
- **Format**: Self-paced with suggested milestones

## What You'll Build

Throughout this course, you'll complete hands-on projects including:
- An AI-powered content creation workflow
- A custom GPT or chatbot for a specific use case
- An AI integration plan for a real-world scenario
- A comprehensive AI ethics framework for your organization
